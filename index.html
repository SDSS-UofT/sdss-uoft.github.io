About Us
Education.
Through our Academic Development Team, we provide academic opportunities and resources for students of all skill levels to learn and grow in the field of data science and statistics.
Career.
Our Professional Development events will provide students exposure to Data Science and Statistics in a professional workplace and give the confidence to continue to pursue a career in this pathway.
Consulting.
Our Consulting Team provides students with the opportunity to work on real-world data science and statistics projects for non-profit organizations and businesses.
Community.
SDSS is dedicated to providing a supportive and positive community at UofT. Our Internal Team establishes the foundation of our community and ensures that our members are able to grow and learn in a safe and welcoming environment.
